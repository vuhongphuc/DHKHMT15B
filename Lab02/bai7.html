A smile is a facial expression formed primarily by flexing the muscles at th sides of mouh. it is believed it takes about 80 muscles to smile. Some smiles include a contraction on the musles at the corner of the eyes, an action known as a "Duchenn smile". Smile performed withou the eye contraction may be perceived as insincere.
